Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp Coconut flour / 6 tbsp Almond flour
  • 1/2 cup Erythritol
  • 3 Eggs
  • 12 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 bar bakers chocolate
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. - Preheat microwave to 325F*
  2. - Mix 1/4 Cocoa + 2 tbsp Coconut flour / 6 tbsp Almond flour + salt
  3. - Mix 3 eggs + 1/2 cup Erythritol + 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  4. - Microwave 1/2 bar bakers chocolate + butter
  5. - Add microwaved chocolate & butter and add mixed eggs and add mixed dry ingredients in one pan
  6. - Make sure microwave is ready
  7. - Put mix in the oven and leave for 55-60 minutes
  8. - Enjoy baking!!!
